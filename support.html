Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please email us at tina@lux-ln.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We'll process your request within 3 business days.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the report feature in the app by tapping the three dots menu on any post or user profile, or email us directly.

How do I set up payments for my business?

Contact us for help with business account setup and payment integration.
