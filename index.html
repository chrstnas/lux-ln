Lightning Payments Made Easy

Lux is a cutting-edge payment app that makes it simple for customers to pay with Bitcoin's Lightning Network at their favorite local shops. With Lux, you can enjoy instant, low-fee transactions and earn dynamic rewards on every purchase!

Key Features:

  • Tap to pay instantly with Lightning or traditional payments
  • Earn up to 11% cashback, automatically boosted for Lightning payments
  • Discover local merchants and their live reward rates on the map
  • Spin the wheel after purchases for a chance to win even more sats back!

Benefits for Merchants:

  • Save on transaction fees and offer automated loyalty programs
  • Attract new customers with Bitcoin and traditional payment options
  • Powerful tools to grow your business and adapt to local trends
