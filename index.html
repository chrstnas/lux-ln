Welcome to Lux: Revolutionizing Everyday Transactions

Lux is more than just an app—it's a movement towards supporting local businesses and enhancing your everyday transactions. Here's how Lux makes a difference for everyone:

For Customers:

  • Support Your Community: Use Lux to shop and eat locally, directly supporting small businesses and boosting your local economy.
  • Earn Cash Back & More: Get instant cash back on your purchases, with the option to receive rewards in Bitcoin or stablecoins.
  • Privacy First: Enjoy transactions with complete privacy. We use secure, anonymous Nostr keys, so your location and personal data remain private.
  • No Credit Card Fees: By using the Bitcoin Lightning Network, we eliminate traditional credit card fees, allowing more of your rewards to stay in your pocket.
  • Engage & Reward: Create your unique profile, interact with merchants, leave reviews, and follow your favorite local spots. Stream money directly to businesses or friends as you please.
  • Exclusive Offers: Discover high-cashback deals and special promotions from local merchants, highlighted for you in the app.

For Merchants:

  • Boost Your Business: Attract more customers by offering cashback rewards and exclusive incentives directly through the app.
  • Save on Fees: Instead of paying traditional credit card or bank fees, use that money to offer cashback directly to your customers. This not only eliminates middlemen but also acts as a powerful marketing tool, enhancing your visibility in the app. For instance, if you'd typically pay a 3% fee to banks, you can instead offer that 3% as cashback, driving more traffic and sales to your business.
  • Flexible Promotions: Tailor your rewards based on your business needs. Offer higher cashback on slower days to boost demand or adjust promotions based on peak times. This flexibility lets you respond dynamically to your business environment.
  • Build Your Brand: Set up a unique business profile, connect with customers, and engage with them through reviews, posts, and special offers.
  • Transparent Transactions: Track sales and customer interactions with complete transparency. We use Nostr keys to maintain privacy while providing valuable insights.

Why Choose Lux?

  • Seamless Transactions: Pay effortlessly with QR codes or NFC, directly through the app.
  • Better Rewards: Enjoy higher cashback rates and more personalized offers compared to traditional methods.
  • Support Local: Directly contribute to your local community by supporting small businesses and keeping money within your neighborhood.
  • Privacy & Security: Experience top-notch privacy and security with Bitcoin and Nostr technology.
  • Growing Network: As Lux scales, you'll gain access to an ever-expanding network of local users, increasing your reach and impact.
